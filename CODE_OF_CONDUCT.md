# Code of Conduct

This project expects respectful, professional collaboration.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus criticism on ideas, wording, or implementation, not people.
- Assume good intent, but be specific about problems.
- Keep discussions relevant to improving the docs and user experience.

## Unacceptable behavior

- harassment, intimidation, or personal attacks
- discriminatory or hateful language
- deliberate disruption of discussions or reviews
- publishing private information without permission

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove comments, issues, or pull requests that violate these standards.

Repeated or severe violations may lead to being blocked from future participation.
